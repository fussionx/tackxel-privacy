Privacy Policy for Quiz Fortune

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Quiz Fortune ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. Quiz Fortune is owned and operated by TACKXEL LTD, a company registered in the United Kingdom.

We respect your privacy. Quiz Fortune does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to play.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not ask for, collect, or store any personally identifiable information directly through the App. There is no sign-up, login, or user profile. Your game progress, coins, scores, and settings are stored locally on your own device and are not transmitted to us.

2. Information Collected by Third-Party Services

To keep the App free and to support its features, we use third-party services that may automatically collect certain information. These services operate under their own privacy policies. They include:

  • Advertising (Google AdMob): We display advertisements, including banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers to show relevant ads and measure performance. You can learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand how the App is used (such as which features are popular and whether the App is performing correctly). This information is collected in aggregate and is not used to personally identify you.

The types of data these services may process can include a device advertising identifier, general device information (such as model and operating system), approximate location derived from IP address, and app usage data. We do not control the data practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (such as coin packs and an option to remove ads). Payments are processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Purchase transactions are handled under the privacy policies of Apple and Google.

4. Children's Privacy

Quiz Fortune is intended for a general audience (ages 12 and up) and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the advertising services in the App, please contact us and we will work with our partners to address it.

5. Your Choices

You can limit ad personalisation through your device settings:

  • On iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.
  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads, where you can reset or limit your advertising ID.

You can also remove advertisements entirely by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option in the App.

6. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data on our own servers, your in-app data remains on your device. We rely on the security measures of Apple, Google, and our third-party service providers for any data they process.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
